Positive Connections

Are you tired of colonies where hostility dominates among your colonists? Despite facing and overcoming many shared challenges, do they still view each other merely as acquaintances?

If so, Positive Connections is the mod you've been looking for.

Seven New Interactions

  • Compliment: Pawns express genuine appreciation for each other's skills and qualities.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Reward your pawns for sharing knowledge, fostering a culture of learning and growth.
  • Gift Giving: Strengthen bonds by exchanging thoughtful gifts, promoting camaraderie.
  • Mediation: Help settle disputes and conflicts peacefully, encouraging harmony.
  • Shared Passions: Bond over mutual hobbies and interests, uniting your colonists.
  • Discuss Ideology: Foster understanding and empathy through meaningful conversations about beliefs.
  • Comfort: A kind pawn, or one in a good mood, will check on another who isn't doing well, offering support after the harsh experiences of the Rim.

While rivalries, insults, and moments of indifference still happen, colonists who can build positive connections will tend to do so, leading to a more harmonious colony. Some interactions may even occur with pawns from other factions, possibly improving diplomatic relations (note: effects on relations are partially implemented).

Note

This mod doesn't add any new graphics. The events are reflected through messages in the upper left-hand corner, as well as in the pawn's interaction log, memories, and mood buffs.

Interactions may cluster, especially early on, but this is a transient effect of pawns feeling inspired and initiating new interactions. Also, note that "gifts" are ephemeral objects and won't appear in your pawn's inventory.

Playtesting and Compatibility

This mod has been playtested for many thousands of hours. However, bugs may still occur, and your reports and suggestions are appreciated.

Combat Extended: Compatible.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Why am I getting a red error about Storytelling?
  • A: Fixed! This bug shouldn't occur anymore.
  • Q: Where are the gifts my colonists are giving each other?
  • A: Gifts in Positive Connections are virtual. It's the thought that counts.
  • Q: Does this mod interact with other mods?
  • A: No, this mod uses simple interactions and is agnostic to other mods or DLC (except for our own mods).
  • Q: What does the gender adjustment switch do?
  • A: It equalizes the chances of men and women initiating relationships. This is now corrected in the base game, but we've kept the option for nostalgia.
  • Q: Does this make the game too easy or hardly change anything?
  • A: This depends on your play style. You can adjust interaction frequency from 1/4 to 10x via mod settings. The recommended value is 1. Positive Connections might also encourage you to explore more challenging difficulty levels.
